# Privacy Policy for Bahiran Delivery Driver App

**Effective Date:** December 3, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Bahiran Delivery Driver App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for delivery services.

By using the Bahiran Delivery Driver App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you register and use our app:

- **Account Information:**
  - Full name (First name and Last name)
  - Phone number
  - Email address
  - Password (encrypted)
  - Profile photo (optional)

- **Financial Information:**
  - Bank account details for withdrawals
  - Transaction history
  - Earnings and balance information
  - Withdrawal requests

- **Identification Information:**
  - Driver license details (if required)
  - Verification documents

### 1.2 Location Data
We collect precise location information to enable delivery services:

- **Foreground Location:** When you're actively using the app to navigate to delivery locations
- **Background Location:** Continuous tracking when you're online and have active deliveries to:
  - Track your real-time position for customers
  - Provide proximity alerts when near pickup/delivery locations
  - Calculate accurate delivery distances and times
  - Optimize delivery routes

**Location Usage:**
- Updates every 5 seconds or 50 meters
- Used for navigation, order tracking, and distance calculation
- Shared with customers when they track their orders
- Stored for delivery history and analytics

### 1.3 Camera and Media
- **Camera Access:** Used exclusively for scanning QR codes for order verification
- **Photo/Media:** For uploading profile pictures or delivery proof (if required)

### 1.4 Device Information
- Device model and operating system
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App version and settings

### 1.5 Usage Data
- Order history and delivery statistics
- App usage patterns and preferences
- Notification settings
- Login/logout times
- Error logs and crash reports

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Delivery
- Process and manage delivery orders
- Navigate to pickup and delivery locations
- Track delivery progress in real-time
- Calculate delivery fees and earnings
- Verify order completion through QR code scanning

### 2.2 Payment and Financial Services
- Process withdrawal requests to your bank account
- Maintain accurate balance and earnings records
- Generate transaction history
- Process tips and delivery fees

### 2.3 Communication
- Send order notifications and alerts
- Provide proximity alerts when near pickup/delivery locations
- Send account updates and important announcements
- Customer support communications
- Push notifications for new orders

### 2.4 Safety and Security
- Verify your identity
- Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
- Investigate and resolve disputes
- Ensure compliance with terms of service
- Monitor app performance and security

### 2.5 Improvement and Analytics
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Optimize delivery routes and efficiency
- Enhance user experience
- Develop new features and services

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal processes

### 3.5 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** All sensitive data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Secure Authentication:** Password hashing and secure token-based authentication
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Security Audits:** Ongoing monitoring and vulnerability assessments

### 4.2 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer for legal/regulatory compliance
- **Transaction Records:** Financial records retained for 7 years as per regulations

### 4.3 Data Storage Location
Your data is stored on secure servers located in:
- Primary servers in Ethiopia/East Africa
- Backup servers for disaster recovery
- Cloud infrastructure with international security standards

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Account Management
You have the right to:
- **Access:** View and download your personal data
- **Update:** Modify your profile information at any time
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Request a copy of your data in portable format

### 5.2 Location Settings
You can control location access:
- Enable/disable location services in device settings
- However, location access is required for core app functionality
- Disabling location will prevent you from accepting and delivering orders

### 5.3 Notification Preferences
You can control notifications:
- Enable/disable sound notifications
- Manage push notification preferences
- Cannot disable critical order-related notifications

### 5.4 Marketing Communications
- Opt-out of promotional messages at any time
- Order-related communications cannot be disabled

## 6. Permissions Required

### 6.1 Android Permissions
- `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` - Precise location for navigation
- `ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION` - Background tracking for active deliveries
- `CAMERA` - QR code scanning for order verification
- `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` - Delivery alerts and notifications
- `VIBRATE` - Alert notifications
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` - Continuous location tracking when online
- `WAKE_LOCK` - Keep app active during deliveries

### 6.2 iOS Permissions
- **Location Always:** For background location tracking
- **Location When In Use:** For navigation during app use
- **Camera:** For QR code scanning
- **Notifications:** For order alerts and updates

### 6.3 Why We Need These Permissions
All permissions are essential for:
- Delivering orders efficiently
- Providing real-time tracking to customers
- Ensuring timely order notifications
- Verifying order completion
- Maintaining service quality
