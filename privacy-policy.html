Introduction

Welcome to Bahiran Delivery Driver App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for delivery services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our app:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, email address, encrypted password, profile photo
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, transaction history, earnings, balance
  • Identification Information: Driver license details (if required), verification documents

1.2 Location Data

üìç Location Tracking

We collect precise location information to enable delivery services:

  • Foreground Location: When actively using the app for navigation
  • Background Location: Continuous tracking when online with active deliveries
  • Updates every 5 seconds or 50 meters
  • Used for navigation, order tracking, distance calculation, and proximity alerts

1.3 Camera and Media

  • Camera Access: Exclusively for scanning QR codes for order verification
  • Photo/Media: Profile pictures or delivery proof (if required)

1.4 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • App version and settings

1.5 Usage Data

  • Order history and delivery statistics
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Login/logout times
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Process and manage delivery orders
  • Navigate to pickup and delivery locations
  • Track delivery progress in real-time
  • Calculate delivery fees and earnings
  • Verify order completion through QR code scanning

2.2 Payment and Financial Services

  • Process withdrawal requests to your bank account
  • Maintain accurate balance and earnings records
  • Generate transaction history
  • Process tips and delivery fees

2.3 Communication

  • Send order notifications and alerts
  • Provide proximity alerts near pickup/delivery locations
  • Send account updates and announcements
  • Customer support communications
  • Push notifications for new orders

2.4 Safety and Security

  • Verify your identity
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Investigate and resolve disputes
  • Ensure compliance with terms of service
  • Monitor app performance and security

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Customers

  • Your name and phone number when you accept an order
  • Real-time location when customer tracks their order
  • Profile information visible to customers

3.2 With Restaurants/Merchants

  • Your name and contact information for order coordination
  • Estimated arrival time at pickup location

3.3 With Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: CBEBirr, Telebirr for processing withdrawals
  • Cloud Services: AWS/Google Cloud for data storage
  • Map Services: Google Maps for navigation
  • Analytics Services: For app performance monitoring

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to court orders, government investigations, or protection of our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing and token-based authentication
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and vulnerability assessments

4.2 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction Records: Financial records retained for 7 years as per regulations

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

You have the right to:

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Update: Modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in portable format

6. Permissions Required

6.1 Android Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION - Precise location for navigation
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION - Background tracking for active deliveries
  • CAMERA - QR code scanning for order verification
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS - Delivery alerts and notifications
  • VIBRATE - Alert notifications
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE - Continuous location tracking when online
  • WAKE_LOCK - Keep app active during deliveries

6.2 iOS Permissions

  • Location Always: For background location tracking
  • Location When In Use: For navigation during app use
  • Camera: For QR code scanning
  • Notifications: For order alerts and updates
